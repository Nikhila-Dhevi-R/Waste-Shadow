# 🌿 Waste Shadow

Waste Shadow is an AI-powered sustainability intelligence platform that uncovers *invisible waste* hidden in everyday consumption. By analyzing bills and product data, it quantifies environmental impact across packaging, production, transport, and water usage—before waste even exists.

Unlike traditional tools that focus on disposal, Waste Shadow enables **preventive sustainability**.

---

##  What Makes It Powerful

-  AI-driven analysis of bills (CSV, PDF, images via OCR)  
-  Invisible Waste Index (IWI) to quantify hidden impact  
-  Live analytics dashboard with waste breakdowns & trends  
-  Future projections (30/90/365 days) for impact forecasting  
-  Actionable recommendations to reduce waste instantly  
-  Water footprint tracking for deeper sustainability insights  

---

##  What You See

- Real-time visible vs invisible waste comparison  
- Category-wise impact (packaging, products, etc.)  
- Waste composition & top contributors  
- Smart low-impact alternatives for everyday items

##  Why It Matters

Most waste is created *before* disposal—during manufacturing, packaging, and logistics. Waste Shadow brings this hidden layer into visibility, empowering users to make **data-driven, low-impact decisions**.
